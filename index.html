Welcome to the my cat's inspiring page. Let's get acquainted - this cat is Kisa

Actually, my cat's first name is Cleopatra. However, when it got to us, it became Anfisa. And after living a little, it became Kisa.

Let's get to know each other better. I will tell a little more about my pet

Kisa was born in 2008 in my cousin's family. It had 2 more sisters and all 3 kittens were sorted at once. I was quite sad, because the kittens were incredibly cute and funny. However, a month later, my future pet was returned. The reason was the energetic and unpredictable behavior of the kitten.

My pet is a Siamese cat with blue eyes. It so happened that Kisa's mother was a white purebred cat, and its father was a real Siamese cat who lived next door. By mixing two breeds together, a very beautiful animal came out, which mesmerized absolutely everyone who came to visit me.

It was absolutely fantastic, in two seconds it climbed onto the curtains on the windows and jumped onto the cabinet that was standing next to it. Her favorite pastime, obviously, was drinking water from the aquarium. We loved doing everything together. We did homework, ate ice cream, slept, ran, had a lot of fun.

It almost never left the apartment and therefore took care of it as if it were its own. Not a single bug or fly stayed with us for long. It organized a real hunt for insects, hid in a hole or jumped like a spider man on the walls. It was even forbidden for pigeons to sit on the other side of the window.

However, it is worth telling that my pet's behavior was sometimes difficult. It did not like guests in the house and reacted to strangers by hissing and running with a bent back. It sensed the veterinarian even at the door of the clinic. And in everyday life, it can easily kick me by paw if it wanted to.

Everyone wanted to stroke it or hold it, but Kisa's strict character was manifested at such moments and it was impossible to do it. Nevertheless, our cat makes every day special. When sad, it starts playing with us. Or, just soak in bed next to a ragged animal.
